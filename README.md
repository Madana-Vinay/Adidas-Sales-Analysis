# Adidas-Sales-Analysis
Analyzed 9,648 Adidas US sales transactions (2020–2021) in Power BI. Built an interactive dashboard tracking $900M revenue, $332M operating profit &amp; 42% operating margin across 6 retailers, 5 US regions, 6 product categories &amp; 3 sales channels. Revealed seasonal trends, regional gaps &amp; top-performing retailer insights to drive strategy.

𝐏𝐫𝐨𝐣𝐞𝐜𝐭 𝐎𝐯𝐞𝐫𝐯𝐢𝐞𝐰

This project presents an end-to-end Sales Performance Analysis of Adidas US operations using Power BI. The dashboard analyzes 9,648 transactions across 2020–2021, covering sales by region, retailer, product category, and sales channel enabling data-driven decisions on revenue optimization, retailer strategy, and market expansion.

𝐎𝐛𝐣𝐞𝐜𝐭𝐢𝐯𝐞

To analyze Adidas US sales data and uncover actionable insights on revenue trends, top-performing retailers, regional performance, and product mix using an interactive Power BI dashboard.
